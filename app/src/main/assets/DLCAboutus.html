About us

DLC-The Learning Club is India’s First Mobile Application developed for catering the needs of English aspirants of Vocabulary and Grammar by Raaah India Education Private Limited.The contents on the application has been developed and are based on the process “Vocabmetry” which is the first and innovative process, invented in the country for memorizing “whole dictionary and whole grammar”.

Raaah India Education Private Limited is a company registered under the laws governing by Government of India for Companies through Registrar of Companies, Gwalior and under the Indian Companies Act 1956 with the registered office at Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. The company is established with a motive to flourish knack for communication & writing and also to grow the competitive edge among the students for future. Pip of the company has been sown in 2011 with the unique and entirely innovative concept of Vocabmetry. The company has been solely involving in providing Vocabulary & Grammar Memorizing course invented by the founder of the company.

The company has emerged as innovative, seasoned, genuine and the most advocated company/institute for carving the communication, writing and competitive skills.

The company is resolute in imparting the knowledge of vocabulary and grammar to the students. Since it is their incipient stage of life and it’s prerequisite to get good command over one of the most spoken languages i.e. English.

The company is providing classes to the students as a part of their curriculum. The company has ameliorated the knack for speaking, writing and competitiveness for thousands of students.

The company began its journey from Bhopal TT Nagar branch and there after it opened its second branch in MP Nagar. The company has got immense love, support and motivation from its beloved students and finally shifted to national capital Delhi. The company has changed its status from Regional class to National level class. The company has taught more than thousands of students and having influence over 100000 students in a year.

The company has made it possible to make students memorize whole dictionary and grammar rules. Earlier that was just a dream of every student, now it’s attainable with DLC. Every student can also memorize dictionary and grammar. There is no need of brilliance and intelligence to memorize dictionary and grammar.
